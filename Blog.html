Shocking Reasons Reading Every Day is Most Beneficial to You

Introduction

In our world today, it is is more common for us to be scrolling endlessly through social media than to take the time to sit with a good book.
Reading may seem a bit old-fashioned and out-of-date for our generation but there's a great assurance that it is one of the most powerful habits we could ever build. As short as 20 minutes per day can transform your mind, lift your mood and shape your future.

  1. Reading Adds To Your Knowledge Bank

    Reading a book is almost like having a conversation with a close friend full of wisdom. Every page has a bout of new information, perspective that you might have never realized or gotten to discover on your own. As the saying goes, "Knowledge is power" - that knowledge is easily gained by the simple act of reading.

  2. Reading Sharpens Your Mind

    Whenever you read, your brain stays active and engaged. Unlike activities like watching TV or playing games, reading makes you imagine, visualize more easily and think from a wider poit of view.

  3. Reading Improves Your Communication Skills

    The more you read, the more words you learn - and so the better you become at expressing yourself. It could be writing an essay or chatting with colleagues or even delivering a presentation, having read as many books as possible helps your vocabulary stand out and you sound more confidentand easy to understand.

These and many more are the reason we should prioritize reading every day for our health, our wealth and our standard of living
